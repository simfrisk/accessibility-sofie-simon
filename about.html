About Web Accessibility

Why is Web Accessibility so important?

Web accessibility ensures that everyone, including people with disabilities, can use websites. It improves usability, expands audience reach, and helps meet legal standards. An accessible web benefits everyone by making online content more inclusive and user-friendly.

Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)

WCAG are guidelines to make web content accessible to people with disabilities. They focus on four principles: Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, and Robust (POUR).

The purpose of ARIA labels

ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) labels provide extra accessibility information for screen readers, helping users with disabilities navigate and interact with web content.
